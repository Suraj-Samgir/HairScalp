About HairScalp

Hair loss and related diseases have been a major issue throughout the world. Hair loss can be a significant concern for many individuals impacting thier life in several ways. A patient may be unable to differentiate between hair fall and hair loss. The goal of this project is to create a web-based platform that uses image processing methods to make it easier to identify or recognize various hair and scalp diseases. The online interface is been built using the Flask framework and other web technologies, and the picture analysis will be performed using Python, Image Processing and Deep Learning Model.

The Problem Statement. It’ll blow your mind.

Indians experience hair loss as a result of aging, stress, medicine, or heredity. Many disorders connected to the hair and scalps are first undetected. A patient may be unable to differentiate between hair fall and hair loss. Hair related disorders take huge time to diagnose since medical and visual tests must be carried out by qualified doctor. As a result, the overall diagnosis is detained, exacerbating the illness's severity. Neural network-based systems are utilized in many fields, including healthcare and health informatics, to anticipate fatal diseases like cancer and tumors because of their capacity for image processing. These apps help patients and doctors and offer a first glimpse at early-stage symptoms. Thus, software that can recognize several types of Hair and Scalp Diseases using Image Processing and other Machine Learning Algorithms will be quite useful. It will be beneficial for all the people suffering from the consequences of some type of issue related to hair and scalp.

Solving the Problem. It’ll Blow Your Mind.


Hair diseases pose a significant global concern, impacting millions worldwide. Conditions like alopecia, dandruff, fungal infections and genetic disorders affect individual's physical appearance, emotional well-being and self-esteem. These disease can be cured when identified and treated at a right stage of time. The individuals may fail to identify the type of the disease they are affected with, thus avoiding the required medications at right moment of time.This problem is solved by the proposed system, which uses images or photographs to automate the process of hair and scalp disease identification. Users are able to input images of scalp and hair, and the algorithm will correctly identify the disease associated with that problem.The sytem returns the desired results to the user which includes the name of the respective disease and the confidence associated with it, that signifies the matching percentage with the actual disease.

Aim of HairScalp

The project's goal is to create a reliable system that uses image processing to automatically identify several diseases related to hair and scalp. This project will offer a user-friendly and precise tool for the identification of hair and scalp diseases by combining Python's image processing capabilities with the Flask framework's web building characteristics. The system's ongoing development and community involvement will benefit the healthcare and hair and scalp hygine industry.
